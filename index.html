Your Data. Your Device.

How Vaulted Roots protects your family history

📱

Stays on your device

Everything is saved locally in your browser. No servers, no cloud, no accounts.

🔐

Encrypted storage

Your tree is encrypted in your device's own storage. We cannot read it, even if we wanted to.

🚫

No tracking

No cookies, no ads. We use Google Analytics anonymously to understand site usage.

📤

You control sharing

Export your tree as a file to share with family. Only send it to people you trust.

🗑️

Delete anytime

Clear your browser data and your tree is gone forever. No backups on our end.

Compare: Other sites upload your family data to their servers and use it to train AI. Vaulted Roots never does this.
